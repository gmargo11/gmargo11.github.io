Gabriel Margolis

I am a PhD student at the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL) at MIT, where I work on Embodied Intelligence. My research, advised by Pulkit Agrawal, is concerned with making effective use of vision in contact-rich control tasks. Previously, I received my BS ('20) and MEng ('21) degrees at MIT.
